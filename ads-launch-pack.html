Plugging in the video reels

Launch on the 10 statics now. When the reels are edited, they go into the same ad set as new ads, so the algorithm compares them against the statics and moves spend to whatever wins.

Recommended: add reels as new ads, keep the statics live. Preserves static learning and lets you read static against video. Pause the weakest statics only once a video has proven out.
